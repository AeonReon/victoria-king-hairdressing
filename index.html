The Studio

A Modern, Five-Star Salon on the North Coast

Victoria King brought her vision to life on Portrush's Eglinton Street — a modern, trendy studio where world-class colour technique meets the relaxed, friendly atmosphere of the North Coast.

With a master colour degree and years of competition experience, Victoria trained herself to the highest standard in balayage, blonding, and technical colouring services. That dedication earned the salon its five-star rating and a loyal client base who travel from across Northern Ireland.
